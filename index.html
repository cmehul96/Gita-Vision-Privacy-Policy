2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • 2.1. Information You Provide Directly:
    • User Content: We collect any notes, bookmarks, and highlights you create within the App. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
    • Communications: If you contact us directly (e.g., for support), we collect your email address, the content of your message, and any attachments.
  • 2.2. Automatically Collected Information:
    • Device Information: We collect information about your mobile device, including the device model, operating system, unique device identifiers (a randomly generated, non-personally identifiable ID used for analytics), and mobile network information (for analytics and troubleshooting). We do not collect your IMEI.
    • Usage Data: We collect anonymous information about how you use the App, such as the verses you read, the features you use (search, bookmarks, etc.), your preferred language, theme settings (light/dark mode, font size), and the time and date of your use. This data is used in an aggregated and anonymized form to improve the App and understand user preferences. We do not track individual reading habits in a personally identifiable way.
    • Offline Content: If you choose to download content for offline access, that content is stored locally on your device.
    • Location Data: We do not collect your precise geolocation data.
    • Crash Reports: We receive automated, anonymized crash reports when the application unexpectedly crashes. This data helps us find bugs and improve the stability of the application. These reports do not contain any personally identifiable information.
  • 2.3. Information from Third Parties:
    • Google Analytics/Firebase: We use Google Analytics/Firebase to collect anonymous usage data and crash reports to help us improve the App. We have configured Google Analytics/Firebase to anonymize IP addresses. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices here: https://policies.google.com/privacy
    • Social Media Sharing: If you choose to share a verse using the built-in sharing functionality, the App will interact with the selected third-party app (e.g., your messaging app, social media app). We do not receive any information back from these third-party apps when you share. The sharing action is initiated entirely on your device.

  • To Improve the App: To analyze anonymous usage patterns, understand user preferences, develop new features, fix bugs, and improve the overall quality and performance of the App.
  • To Communicate with You: To respond to your inquiries, provide customer support, and send you important updates and notifications about the App (e.g., updates about new features or bug fixes). We will not send you marketing emails.
  • For Security Purposes: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security vulnerabilities.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: We will only share your information with third parties if you have given us your explicit consent to do so.
  • With Service Providers: We use Google (Firebase/Analytics) as a service provider for analytics and crash reporting, as described above. Google is contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only as we have instructed, in accordance with their privacy policy.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request, such as a subpoena, court order, or government investigation.
  • In Connection with a Business Transaction: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, sale of assets, or other business transaction, anonymized usage data and crash reports may be transferred as part of that transaction. Your locally stored data (bookmarks, notes, highlights) would not be part of such a transfer, as it is stored only on your device. We will notify you via a prominent notice on our App of any change in ownership that affects the handling of anonymized analytics data.
  • Aggregated or De-identified Data: We may share aggregated or de-identified usage data (which cannot reasonably be used to identify you) with third parties for research, analytics, or other purposes.
  • No Advertising Networks: We do not share your information with any advertising networks. The App is ad-free.

  • We limit access to anonymous usage data to authorized personnel only.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.
